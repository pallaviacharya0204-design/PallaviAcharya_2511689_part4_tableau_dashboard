# Overall Business Summary

The Tableau dashboard indicates healthy sales performance with opportunities to improve profitability, shipping efficiency, and return management. Regional and category-level analysis helps identify where resources should be focused. Monitoring discount policies, customer segments, and delivery performance will enable management to make more informed, data-driven decisions and support long-term business growth.
